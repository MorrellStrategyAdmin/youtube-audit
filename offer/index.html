Common questions

How much of my time does this take?

Far less than running it yourself. Just the parts that genuinely need you: your unique insights, recording, and quick reviews. Expect around 2 to 4 hours per week.

Is this just another agency?

No. Most agencies run the same playbook on every account. We do the strategic work ourselves, build everything around your specific business, and give you the choice of staffing your own team or having us run it.

Do you use AI to write the content?

No. The strategy and the ideas come directly from us and our billions of views of experience. We only use tools to make production faster, not to replace thinking.

What happens after 90 days?

You have a working channel and a system that runs. Whether we built your internal team or ran it as your agency, you are left with positioning, SOPs, and a repeatable process, not a dependency that collapses when we step back.

How quickly will I see results?

It depends on what results mean to you. Booking your first sales calls can happen from your first video; reaching a hundred thousand subscribers from zero takes much longer. With most people we work with, we'd expect you to start booking calls and closing deals within the first 90 days.

Do you write all the scripts?

Rarely. If you're the subject matter expert, other people shouldn't be injecting their knowledge and framing it as yours. We script the parts that don't need your expertise, where appropriate, and many formats (like vlogs) need little scripting at all.

What if I already have editors or thumbnail designers?

If you'd rather keep your current production team and have us focus on strategy, ideation, scripting, and directing them, that's an option. We'd just need to make sure they can deliver the quality required to succeed.
