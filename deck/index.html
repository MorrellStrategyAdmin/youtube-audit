What's holding your channel back

It's almost always one of these five.

01

No reach

A few hundred views a video. Nothing to convert, and nothing to build on.

02

No differentiation

Made without a real strategy or point of view, so it never breaks out.

03

No system

No ideation or packaging process. Every video starts from a blank page.

04

Depends on you

Nothing ships unless you're in the weeds with the editor.

05

Views, no business

All top of the funnel. Plenty of views, but no clients.

Most people guess wrong about which one is actually theirs.

  • Read the five quickly, don't labour each.
  • Then mirror back their own words from the questions: "From what you told me, yours is really #2 and #5. You get some views, but it isn't turning into calls."
    • ★ Takeaway #1: name their real bottleneck out loud. This is the first thing they keep even if they never buy. Let them agree before you move on.
  • Silence is fine. About 60 to 75 seconds.
